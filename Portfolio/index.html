Lung Capacity Indicator Device

This innovative IoT-based biomedical project offers real-time insights into respiratory wellness, empowering users to track and optimize their lung function with ease. By blowing air into the spirometer, internal balls rise, passing sensors at specific distances. Utilizing Arduino Uno, male-female wires, and LEDs, the device accurately measures lung capacity.
